Programme de recherche en partenariat : Études scientifiques sur la connectivité - Synthèse des connaissances

Explore our research recommendations and findings for Quebec's connectivity policy.

Overview

The Programme de recherche en partenariat : Études scientifiques sur la connectivité – Synthèse des connaissances is jointly offered by the Secrétariat à l’Internet haute vitesse et aux projets spéciaux de connectivité (SIHV) and the Fonds de recherche du Québec (FRQ). The program promotes partnerships between universities, economic and governmental partners, and applied research users. Through the mobilization of experts in various fields across Quebec's academic landscape, this program aims to produce actionable knowledge to support SIHV in its advisory role.

Timeline

  • Result Announcement: March 2025

  • Call Year: 2025–2026
  • Program Link: FRQ Official Page

Key Features

  • Total available funding (excluding FIR): $44,880
  • Each grant can reach up to $22,440 over 4 months
  • At least one team member must belong to the Nature and Technologies sector (NT)
  • Each proposal must address at least two of the following six axes:
  • Axis 1: Technological
  • Axis 2: Social Impacts
  • Axis 3: Environmental Impacts
  • Axis 4: Health Impacts on Humans and Animals
  • Axis 5: Network Resilience and Risk Management
  • Axis 6: Legal and Regulatory Aspects

The resulting knowledge synthesis must include a comprehensive and updated literature review highlighting research gaps and future directions within the selected axes.
